Vinnies Pizza



Vinnies Pizza got its start as a tiny hole in the wall style pizza joint in 1977. The pizza joint quickly became a town favorite and soon had plenty of customers. The high demand led to the need for a bigger location with seating. Vinnie's then moved to where it is located now on Canton Street in 1982. Vinnies II opened in the spring of 2019 and now provides a full authentic Italian style menu right next door.
